Description

Danurb Tours is a fictitious travel agency working on real places. Our mission is to bring the individual countries of the region together to offer touristic destinations on a common denominator: the river. Our crew did their best to select sites of cultural and natural heritage little represented in the public mind and label them through themes intriguing enough. The five routes we offer establish a network of interconnected places and stories multi-ethnic in origin, turbulent in historical hindsight. Still, we believe the centuries old coexistence on the riverbanks of the Danube has already forged an identity „aquatic” we can all share and cherish. What if you followed one of our itineraries this summer already?
